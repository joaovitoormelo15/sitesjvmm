curiosidades

1 - The nail is what separates humans from other mammals In the mammalian group, only humans and primates have nails. The rest display claws, which are necessary for their daily tasks. 2 - Fingernails grow an average of 3.5 millimeters per month Hand nails have an average growth of 3.5 millimeters per month, but this can vary according to the fingers. The middle nail grows the fastest, while the thumb nail grows the slowest. However, they all tend to grow more during the summer and those on the hands faster than those on the feet. 3 - Men's nails grow longer than women's The data may even cause a certain strangeness, but the American Academy of Dermatology proved that men's nails grow faster than women's. This order only changes during pregnancy, when nails tend to grow faster. 4 - Cutting your nails often helps them grow You know that common comment in beauty salons that cutting the ends of the hair helps make it grow faster? The theory also applies to nails. Therefore, to have beautiful and long nails, it is better to start cutting them frequently now. By the way, nails are also made of keratin, the protein in hair strands.
